UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
DESCRIPTION

The United States of America (USA), generally known as the United States or America, is a nation predominantly situated in North America, composed of 50 states, a federal region, five main self-government zones and numerous possessions. At 3.8 million square miles, it is the third or fourth largest country in the world by total size. With a population of more than 328 million, it is the third most populated nation in the world. The national capital is Washington, D.C., and New York City is the most populous city.
