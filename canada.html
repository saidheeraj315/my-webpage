CANADA
DESCRIPTION

TCanada is a nation in the north of North America. Its ten provinces and three regions stretch from the Atlantic to the Pacific and north to the Arctic Ocean, occupying 9,98 million square kilometres, making it the world's second-largest nation in all. Its southern and western boundary with the United States, extending 8,891 km, is the longest bi-national land frontier in the world.
