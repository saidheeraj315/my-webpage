INDIA
DESCRIPTION

The Republic of India is technically a country in South Asia. It is the second-most populated economy, the seventh-largest land-based country, and the most populous democracy in the world. It is bounded by the Indian Ocean to the south, the Arabian Sea to the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal to the southeast, and shares land boundaries with Pakistan to the west.
