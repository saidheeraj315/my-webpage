Officially, Italy The Italian Republic is a nation made up of a peninsula delimited by the Alps with numerous islands surrounding it. Italy is situated in South Central Europe and is considered part of Western Europe. The Unified Parliamentary Republic, with Rome as its capital, occupies a total area of 301,340 km2 and shares land boundaries with France, Switzerland, Austria and Slovenia..
