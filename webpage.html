About covid 19:

COVID-19 is a new coronavirus disease that appeared in China in December 2019. The new coronavirus can be transmitted from person to person. A laboratory test is diagnosed.

Researchers now realise that the latest coronavirus spreads by respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs or sneezes.
